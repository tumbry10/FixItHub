Welcome to the Community Issue Tracker & Resource Hub

FixItHub is a community-driven fault reporting system built to help local residents to report issues rising in their residential area to their service providers and get assistance. It allows users to report issues (e.g., infrastructure faults), track their status, and get assistance from service providers. It's designed to streamline communication between local residents and service providers.
